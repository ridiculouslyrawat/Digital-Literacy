# Social Media Best Practices: The Dos and Don'ts

## The Dos

* **Audit your privacy settings:** Check your accounts regularly to ensure you know exactly who can see your posts and tagged photos.
* **Network professionally:** Connect with professors, alumni, and industry leaders on platforms like LinkedIn.
* **Think before you "Share":** Verify the source of news or information before reposting to avoid spreading misinformation.
* **Highlight your achievements:** Post about Dean's List honors, club involvements, or volunteer work to build a positive digital trail.
* **Use "The Grandma Test":** If you wouldn't want your grandmother (or a future hiring manager) to see it, don't post it.

---

## The Don'ts

* **Don't post illegal or illicit content:** This includes underage drinking or drug use, which can violate student codes of conduct.
* **Don't engage in "keyboard warrior" behavior:** Avoid online bullying. Screenshots are permanent even if you delete the comment.
* **Don't complain about your internship or job online:** Venting about a "boring" boss can lead to immediate termination.
* **Don't share sensitive personal info:** Keep details like your dorm room number, class schedule, or birthdate private, as sharing them increases identity theft risks.
* **Don't overshare during a crisis:** Posting highly emotional or private details can lead to "oversharer's remorse" later.
